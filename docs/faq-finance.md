## Expense Claims

### How do I submit an expense claim?

Log into the HR portal at `hr.internal`, navigate to **Finance > Expense Claims**, and complete the claim form. Attach itemised receipts for all expenses. Your line manager will receive a notification to approve or reject the claim. See `policy-expenses.md` for the full expense policy.

### When will my expense claim be reimbursed?

Approved claims are processed in the monthly payroll run. Claims approved before the 10th of the month will be included in that month's payment. Claims approved after the 10th will roll over to the following month. Payment is made on the 15th of each month.

### My claim was rejected — what do I do?

You will receive an email notification with the reason for rejection. Common reasons include missing receipts, exceeding policy limits, or missing pre-approval. You can resubmit a corrected claim via the HR portal. If you believe the rejection was made in error, contact the Finance team at finance@company.com.

### Can I claim expenses in foreign currency?

Yes. Foreign currency expenses are converted to AUD at the exchange rate on the date the expense was incurred. You must include the original receipt showing the foreign currency amount. The HR portal will apply the conversion automatically when you enter the currency and date.

### I lost my receipt — can I still claim?

For expenses under AUD $25, a statutory declaration may be accepted as a substitute. For expenses AUD $25 and above, original receipts are required. Contact finance@company.com in advance of submitting if you have lost a receipt for a large expense.

## Invoices and Purchase Orders

### How do I raise a purchase order?

Purchase orders for amounts under AUD $5,000 can be raised by your department manager via the Finance system at `finance.internal`. Purchase orders above AUD $5,000 require Head of Finance approval. Contact finance@company.com to initiate.

### A supplier has sent me an invoice directly — what should I do?

Forward the invoice to accounts@company.com immediately. Do not make payment directly. All supplier payments are processed centrally by the Finance team. Include the relevant purchase order number in your email.

### How long does it take to pay a supplier invoice?

Standard payment terms are 30 days from the date of invoice receipt by the Finance team. Urgent payments can be expedited with Head of Finance approval. Contact finance@company.com with the invoice and reason for urgency.

## Budgets and Financial Reporting

### How do I find out my department's budget?

Department budget summaries are available in the Finance system under **Reporting > Department Budgets**. Access is restricted to department heads and their designated finance liaisons. If you require access, contact finance@company.com.

### How do I request a budget increase?

Budget increase requests must be submitted to the Head of Finance with a written business justification at least 2 weeks before the expenditure is planned. Requests are reviewed at the monthly Finance Committee meeting.

### When is the financial year end?

ACME Corporation's financial year runs from 1 July to 30 June. The final month of each quarter (September, December, March, June) is subject to a deployment blackout — see `runbook-deploy.md` — and increased Finance team workload. Non-urgent finance queries during these periods may experience delays.

### How do I access monthly financial reports?

Monthly P&L and department cost centre reports are distributed to department heads by the Finance team within 10 business days of month end. Historical reports are available in the Finance system under **Reporting > Monthly Reports**.

## Payroll

### When is payday?

Salaries are paid on the 15th of each month. If the 15th falls on a weekend or public holiday, payment is made on the preceding business day.

### How do I update my bank account details for payroll?

Log into the HR portal and navigate to **My Details > Banking**. Changes must be submitted at least 5 business days before the next pay date to take effect that month. If you miss the cutoff, the change will apply from the following month.

### I think there is an error in my payslip — what should I do?

Contact payroll@company.com with your employee ID and a description of the discrepancy. Payroll queries are responded to within 2 business days. If an underpayment is confirmed, a correction will be made in the next available payroll run or as an off-cycle payment if the amount is significant.

### How do I access my payment summaries for tax purposes?

Annual payment summaries are available in the HR portal under **Pay > Payment Summaries** after 14 July each year. If you require an amended payment summary, contact payroll@company.com.
